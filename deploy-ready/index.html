泰兴市滨江机械科技有限公司成立于 2013 年，位于江苏省泰兴市滨江镇，是一家专注于机床刻度盘、标牌和刻度尺研发与制造的精密机械企业。公司以精密刻线技术为核心竞争力，产品涵盖车床、磨床、钻床、铣床、镗床、线切割等多种机床配套刻度盘，并提供金属打标和无光镀铬等表面加工服务。

公司秉承"精益求精、客户至上"的经营理念，从设计到生产再到调试，为客户提供完整的一站式服务，赢得了众多机床制造企业的长期信赖。纳税信用等级被评为 A 级，彰显了企业的诚信经营与稳健发展。

Taixing Binjiang Machinery Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 2013, located in Binjiang Town, Taixing City, Jiangsu Province. The company specializes in the R&D and manufacturing of machine tool graduated discs, signage, and graduated scales. With precision engraving technology as our core competence, our products cover graduated discs for lathes, grinding machines, drilling machines, milling machines, boring machines, wire cutting machines, and more — along with metal marking and matte chrome plating services.

Guided by the philosophy of "pursuing excellence, customer first," we provide complete one-stop services from design through production to commissioning, earning the long-term trust of numerous machine tool manufacturers. Our A-grade tax credit rating reflects our commitment to honest business practices and steady growth.
